Fun Microbiome Facts

Gut vs Human Cells

Your gut has more bacterial cells than your human cells!

Skin Microbiome

Skin microbiome changes every time you shower.

Oral Microbiome

The oral microbiome has over 700 different bacterial species!

Influence on Mood

Your microbiome can influence your mood and behavior.

Microbiome and Body Weight

About 10% of your body weight is made up of bacteria!

Unique Fingerprint

Each person's microbiome is unique, like a fingerprint.

Gut and Immune System

The gut microbiome is involved in regulating metabolism and the immune system.

Gut Protection

Good bacteria in your gut can help protect against harmful pathogens.

Gut Health & Immunity

The diversity of microbiota in your gut is a key factor in maintaining good health.

Immune System Connection

About 70% of your immune system is located in your gut, heavily influenced by your microbiome.
